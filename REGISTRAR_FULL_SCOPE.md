# Comprehensive Registrar Module Scope (NEP 2020 Compliant)

The Registrar's Office is the backbone of University administration. Below is a comprehensive list of modules and features required for a complete system.

## 1. Admissions & Enrollment (Current Status: Implemented)
- **Online Application:** Registration forms, document uploads, fee payment gateway integration.
- **Seat Allocation:** Merit list generation, quota management (SC/ST/OBC/EWS/Sports).
- **Lateral Entry:** Handling Diploma to Degree direct admissions.
- **International Admissions:** Visa handling, equivalence certificates.
- **Enrollment:** Generating permanent Enrollment Numbers/Roll Numbers.
- **ID Cards:** Generation of digital and physical ID cards.

## 2. Student Lifecycle Management (Current Status: Partially Implemented)
- **Status Tracking:** Active, Detained, Suspended, Withdrawn, Alumni.
- **Section/Batching:** Assigning students to specific sections/practical groups.
- **Subject Selection:** Elective / Open Elective choices (CBCS/NEP).
- **Promotion/Detention:** Year-end processing based on Exam results.

## 3. Academic Configuration
- **Program Structure:** Define Schools, Departments, Programs (UG/PG/PhD).
- **Curriculum/Syllabus:** Define subjects, credits, prerequisites.
- **Academic Calendar:** Start/End dates, Holidays, Exam windows.
- **Timetabling:** Class and Room scheduling.

## 4. Certification & Records (Focus Area: Convocation)
- **Convocation Management:**
    - List of Eligible Graduands.
    - Convocation Registration (Gown booking, attendance).
    - **Degree Certificate Generation:** Printing degrees with security features (QR, Holo).
    - Medalist/Rank Holder List.
- **Document Services:**
    - Provisional Degree Certificates (PDC).
    - Migration Certificates.
    - Transfer Certificates (TC) / College Leaving Certificate (CLC).
    - Bonafide / Character Certificates.
    - Transcripts.
- **Verification Service:** Third-party verification for background checks.

## 5. Examination Interface (Registrar's View)
- **Tabulation Register (TR):** Permanent record of marks/grades.
- **Marksheets:** Printing and distribution logs.
- **Re-evaluation:** Handling administrative requests for result review.

## 6. NEP 2020 Compliance Features
- **ABC (Academic Bank of Credits):** Integration for credit transfer/accumulation.
- **Multiple Entry/Exit:** Handling Certificate (1 yr), Diploma (2 yr), Degree (3/4 yr) exits.
- **ODL / Online:** Tracking online credit courses (SWAYAM/NPTEL).

## 7. Regulatory & Reporting
- **Statutory Reports:** UGC, AICTE, AISHE, NIRF data generation.
- **Internal Reporting:** Gender parity, intake vs filled seats, dropout rates.

## 8. Alumni Affairs
- **Alumni Registration:** Database of passed-out students.
- **Alumni Portal:** Job postings, reunions, donations.

## 9. General Administration
- **RTI Management:** Tracking and responding to Right to Information requests.
- **Grievance Redressal:** Handling student/parent complaints.
- **Notices & Circulars:** Official communication channel.
